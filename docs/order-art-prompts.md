# Order Art — Image Generation Prompts

## Style Prefix (use for all prompts)

> Black and white ink illustration on a pure white background, hand-drawn field journal sketch style. Fine crosshatching and pen strokes for shading, no solid fills. Detailed linework with visible hatching texture. Fantasy naturalist field study aesthetic. No colour, no gradients, no digital effects. Transparent/white background. RGBA PNG.

## Header Images (800 x 920px)

### The Warpack

> A war banner planted in cracked earth, its tattered fabric showing a snarling wolf skull sigil. Around its base: a collection of notched blades, severed trophy teeth strung on leather cord, and claw marks gouged into the banner pole. Scattered wolf paw prints in the dirt. A few javelins and a crude axe lean against the pole. The scene suggests a nomadic war camp — no people, just the evidence of a hunting pack. Fine ink crosshatching, black and white field sketch on white background.

### The Gilded Hand

> An ornate merchant's ledger lying open on a stone surface, its pages covered in dense handwritten entries. A set of precision scales sits beside it, one pan holding stacked coins, the other holding a single polished gemstone. A wax seal stamp bearing an open-palm sigil rests nearby, next to a spilled pouch of gold coins. A quill pen in an inkwell. Fine chains and a magnifying loupe complete the scene. Fine ink crosshatching, black and white field sketch on white background.

### The Runeforged

> A runesmith's workbench viewed from above at a slight angle. A large die sits at the centre, its faces carved with intricate arcane runes that seem to glow (suggested by radiating hatch lines). Around it: scattered runic diagrams on parchment scraps, a chisel and engraving tools, a magnifying lens, and fragments of crystallised energy. Geometric sigils are etched into the workbench surface itself. Fine ink crosshatching, black and white field sketch on white background.

### The Brood

> A cluster of fungal growths erupting from a cracked stone floor. Thick mycelia tendrils spread outward like veins. Several bulbous spore pods hang from stalks at varying heights, some split open releasing clouds of spores (rendered as stippled dots). A coiled serpent skeleton is half-consumed by the growth, bones visible through the fungal mass. Drops of venom bead on thorned stems. Fine ink crosshatching, black and white field sketch on white background.

### The Ironward

> A battered tower shield planted upright in stone, bearing a simple crest of crossed bones behind a helm visor. Deep sword cuts and dents scar its surface. Wrapped around the shield's base: linen bandages stained with use, a clay flask of healing salve, and a worn leather belt with a buckle shaped like a closed fist. Behind the shield, a cracked stone wall with the Ironward's sigil chiselled into it. Fine ink crosshatching, black and white field sketch on white background.

## Guild Badge Images (400 x 400px)

### The Warpack

> A circular guild badge: a snarling wolf skull viewed head-on, jaws open, with two crossed short swords behind it. Surrounded by a ring of claw marks scratched into the border. Rough, primal linework. Black and white ink sketch, white background, no colour.

### The Gilded Hand

> A circular guild badge: an open palm facing outward, fingers spread, with a single coin floating above the palm. Surrounded by a thin decorative border of interlocking chain links. Clean, precise linework. Black and white ink sketch, white background, no colour.

### The Runeforged

> A circular guild badge: a six-sided die viewed at a three-quarter angle, its visible faces inscribed with arcane runes. Radiating lines suggest energy emanating from the die. Surrounded by a geometric border of interlocking angular patterns. Precise, technical linework. Black and white ink sketch, white background, no colour.

### The Brood

> A circular guild badge: a coiled serpent wrapped around a central mushroom, the serpent's mouth open with venom dripping from its fangs. Small spore dots drift from the mushroom cap. Surrounded by a border of intertwined thorned vines. Organic, textured linework. Black and white ink sketch, white background, no colour.

### The Ironward

> A circular guild badge: a tower shield viewed straight-on, bearing a helm visor crest at its centre. A horizontal crack runs across the shield — damaged but unbroken. Surrounded by a thick border with a bandage-wrap texture. Sturdy, bold linework. Black and white ink sketch, white background, no colour.
